Connecting the digital and
physical worlds of fandom
Goodz are digitally-enhanced wooden
collectibles that provide fans with a
commemorative, physical connection
to their favorite artists.
Technology
Goodz use the NFC technology built into all
modern smartphones to let fans simply tap
collectible objects to be taken directly to
digital content:
● Albums/playlists on streaming platforms
● Live concert recordings
● Exclusive merch or ticket offers
● Press, media kits, or artist linktrees
Collecting around music is more
popular than ever, but most fans don’t
need or use outdated media anymore.
Goodz are an affordable, functional,
environmentally friendly alternative to
traditional media in a world where:
● 50% of vinyl purchasers don’t
even own a record player
● 75% of millennials say they
consider sustainability when
making a physical purchase
● Goodz are made from fast-growing, sustainable basswood and printed with
VOC-free UV inks
● Our crates are produced from 100% recycled PLA, a biodegradable,
carbon-neutral plastic
● We prioritize recycled and biodegradable materials in all of our packaging
