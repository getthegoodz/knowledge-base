Goodz: Internal GPT Knowledge Base
Overview Goodz is a startup that creates collectible physical objects—called Goodz—that unlock exclusive digital content when tapped with a phone. These objects are NFC-enabled, often made of wood, and serve as modern, sustainable alternatives to outdated physical media like CDs or vinyl records. Each Goodz acts as a key to an exclusive digital experience, including music, photos, playlists, videos, chats, or microsites.
Goodz has two primary business lines: 1. D2C (Direct-to-Consumer) via its Shopify store. 2. B2B (Business-to-Business) for artists, labels, events, and brands who want to produce custom Goodz for their communities.
Core Products - Mixtape Goodz: The best-selling product. A customizable wooden collectible that links to a digital playlist, album, or other media. - Authenticator Goodz: Uses NTAG424 chips to gate premium, exclusive content. Often used in deluxe album drops or limited-edition campaigns. - Crates: Bundles of themed Goodz (e.g. Original Jazz Classics Crate Vol. 1). Sold as collectible sets with exclusive content.
Technology Stack - NFC Chips: NTAG213 (standard) or NTAG424 (for authentication). - Dynamic Link Platform: When tapped, the NFC directs to a unique webpage that verifies the tap and grants content access. - Backend: Custom CMS and SaaS tools to manage links, content, and analytics. - Microsites: Lightweight, mobile-friendly experiences hosted and maintained by Goodz.
Brand Voice & Positioning - Tone: Clever, nostalgic, warm, music-loving. Avoid overly techy language. Focus on fan connection. - Mission: Bridge the digital and physical worlds for fans, artists, and collectors. - Core Message: Goodz are modern keepsakes. Tap to unlock exclusive content. Perfect for superfans, gifts, and moments that matter. - Visual Identity: Retro-influenced, bold, playful. Crate-digger aesthetic meets modern minimalism.
Sustainability - Materials: Basswood for collectibles (rapid-growth, sustainable). Recycled PLA for crates. VOC-free UV printing process. - Packaging: Recycled and biodegradable whenever possible.
Milestones & Credibility - 13,000+ units sold B2B - Clients include Grammy-winning artists, Warner Music, Sony Music - 25% repeat purchase rate on Shopify - Partnerships with festivals, agencies, and music labels - Official Britney Spears release for “Oops!… I Did It Again” 25th Anniversary
Use Cases - Music Releases: Deluxe physical drops with digital content. - Events: Festival and conference swag that unlocks exclusive media or discounts. - Weddings & Celebrations: Custom crates as party favors linking to playlists or photo albums. - Gifting: Holiday or birthday gifts with personalized mixtapes or messages.
Competitive Advantage - First-mover advantage in NFC-enabled collectibles. - Patent-pending dynamic linking system. - Eco-friendly production. - Highly flexible across industries: music, events, sports, creator economy.
Team - Founders: Mike Rosenthal & Chris Cerrito (also active in Fanaply, another startup) - Manufacturing: Based in China - Operations: U.S.-based, focused on marketing, partnerships, and fulfillment.
Business Model - D2C: High AOV (~$37), seasonal surges (especially Q4), bestsellers are Mixtape Goodz and Crates. - B2B: Wholesale orders from record labels, event organizers, weddings, and brand campaigns. Often includes setup fees and design work. - SaaS (in development): Partners will be able to create and manage digital content flows themselves, with analytics and user management.
Legal / IP - Provisional patent filed (June 2023): “NFC-Enabled Physical Objects for Dynamic Digital Content Interaction and Enhanced User Engagement” - All album artwork used under license - Terms of Service and Privacy Policy maintained for user-facing platforms
Key Terminology - Goodz: Always plural, never singular. Each is a collectible object with NFC tech. - Unlocker: Internal nickname for NTAG424-enabled chips that authenticate the tap. - Microsite: The digital destination Goodz link to. Often includes media, custom messaging, or exclusive downloads. - Crate: A themed set of multiple Goodz (usually 3 or 5).
