Questions people will have

Do I need an app?

No. The idea should stay lightweight: tap with a phone, open the music experience, enjoy it.

Do I need Spotify?

No. Spotify import should be optional. Manual links should still work so we do not create conversion friction.

Do they come preloaded?

The new sales flow should let buyers choose what they want on each Good before purchase, even if the backend setup remains flexible.

How fast do they ship?

Current ops suggest a few days for normal D2C fulfillment, which is an important confidence-builder to keep visible.
