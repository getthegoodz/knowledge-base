-- 1 of 11 --

Goodz are
digitally-enabled
physical objects that
connect fans to gated
online content

-- 2 of 11 --

Fans simply tap them with their phone to
access curated content that can include:
❖ Albums, singles, or playlists
❖ Music videos
❖ Live concert recordings
❖ Exclusive merch offers
❖ Ticket presales
❖ Superfan-only content

-- 3 of 11 --

Made from sustainable basswood using
non-VOC printing, they are an eco-friendly
alternative to traditional media in a world where:
❖ 50% of vinyl purchasers don’t even own a
record player
❖ 75% of millennials say they consider
sustainability when making a physical
purchase

-- 4 of 11 --

Nostalgia for physical
objects amongst
Millennials and Gen Z is
at an all-time high.
Goodz satisfy this need
while connecting these
analog objects to online
content to create a
powerful, hybrid
experience.

-- 5 of 11 --

Some ways they are being used:
❖ Collectible box sets - A custom mini crate full of albums from an
artist or record label
❖ Album/song download cards - A less expensive, more functional,
and eco-friendly alternative to CDs, cassettes, and vinyl
❖ Gating exclusive content - User must be holding Goodz to access
music, videos, and other content
❖ Live concert recordings - Sell them before/during a show and
upload the recording afterwards so fans can take the show home
❖ Promotional giveaways - Label samplers, priority tracks, sponsored
content, and more can be given away on collectible Goodz
