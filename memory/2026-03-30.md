### Strategic conclusions Janet wrote up

Main thesis: Goodz should not just make Shopify checkout "more interesting". The real opportunity is to move value creation upstream of checkout.

The strongest product directions identified:

1. **Consumer mixtape / gift builder**
   - A pre-checkout creation flow where someone makes a personalized Good around a playlist/album/message/photos.
   - This makes Goodz legible as a gift-creation experience, not just a product SKU.

2. **Spotify OAuth as optional enhancement, not gating step**
   - Good use: import playlists, saved albums, recent listening, prefill choices.
   - Bad use: requiring login to buy.
   - Best pattern: paste URL fast path + optional "Connect Spotify" enhanced path.

3. **Wedding / event configurator**
   - Dedicated flow by event type, quantity, use case, art upload, landing-page type, estimate range, and timeline.
   - Strong fit because the site already explicitly mentions weddings and events.

4. **B2B bulk / campaign flow**
   - Better framed as ordering a branded activation rather than buying a standard product.
   - Strong candidate segments: musicians/labels, DJs/playlisters, wedding planners, event agencies, conferences, hospitality, restaurants/bars, museums, nonprofits, photographers, corporate gifting.

5. **Artist/fan flow with stronger collector mechanics**
   - Emphasize scarcity, exclusivity, bundles, numbered editions, and fan unlocks.
