# Goodz Onboarding Questions for Mike

This is a living list of high-value questions Janet should use to learn the real business over time.

## Current priority batch

1. What parts of Goodz are truly operational today?
   - website
   - Shopify store
   - custom production
   - landing-page/content tech
   - analytics
   - fulfillment
   - sales/outreach
2. What is the biggest reason Goodz has not yet become primary-income scale?
3. What current opportunities, conversations, or relationships feel warmest right now?
4. If we built an indie-artist flow first, what is the minimum viable version that would feel sellable to you?
5. Of the possible indie-artist subsegments (DJs, touring indie bands, strong-Bandcamp artists, small labels), which are most practically targetable right now via ads, outreach, communities, or existing channels?

## Future question areas

- Margins by product line
- Best historical customers and why they bought
- Repeatability of B2B custom work
- Which partnerships are realistic to revive
- Which old plans are still real versus stale deck material
- What Mike most wants Janet to own autonomously versus always bring back for discussion
