02 — Target Audience

Who Buys Custom Goodz

Persona A: The Touring Indie Band
Primary buyer — 60% of addressable market
Profile
2–5 members, 500–10K monthly Spotify listeners
Touring
15–50 shows/year, 100–500-cap rooms
Merch
Self-managed or boutique management; self-run via Bandcamp + Venmo/ShopPay
Pain
Merch table looks like everyone else's. Fans walk past. Nothing stands out.
Want
Something fans actually keep and use. Revenue that justifies table space.
Price Sens.
$172 upfront (50 units) is accessible — can't afford $2K+ runs
Persona B: Bedroom-to-Bandcamp Artist
Secondary buyer — emerging artists, digital audiences
Profile
Solo or small group, primarily digital, 1K–50K Bandcamp listeners
Touring
Occasional shows, no formal merch operation yet
Merch
Bandcamp storefront + occasional local shows
Pain
Wants to give super-fans something physical but doesn't know what to produce
Want
Low-risk way to test physical merch. Something fans actually value.
Price Sens.
Highly sensitive — $172 is the minimum viable test
